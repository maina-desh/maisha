Rules & Regulations

1. Membership Eligibility

All members must be above 18 years old and provide valid identification.

2. Monthly Contributions

Each member must contribute a minimum of Ksh 500 per month to remain active.

3. Loan Eligibility

Loans are available to members who have contributed consistently for at least 6 months.

4. Late Payment Penalties

Late contributions attract a penalty of Ksh 50 per month.

5. Member Code of Conduct

Members must maintain professionalism and integrity in all Sacco dealings.

6. Withdrawal Policy

Members who wish to withdraw must give a 30-day notice and settle any pending balances.

7. Dispute Resolution

Any disputes arising within the Sacco will be resolved by a committee appointed by the board.
